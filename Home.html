IAN FRYE

is a drummer, singer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist from the DC metropolitan area currently based in Fredericksburg, VA. He formerly played drums and sang in the DC-based jam band Surprise Attack who, over the past few years, have played at larger venues and festivals on the east coast including Lockn' Festival in 2019. Ian's drumming style is centered around funk, rock, and r&b and his songwriting influences include The Meters, Talking Heads, Grateful Dead, Stevie Wonder, The Band, Dopapod, Kendrick Lamar and Herbie Hancock. He has written several songs for Surprise Attack and continues to compose on a regular basis.
